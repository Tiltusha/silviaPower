ENGINE SPECIFICATION

The Nissan SR20DET is a 2.0 liter (1998 cc) inline four-cylinder petrol internal combustion engine that is part of the SR family. Serially produced from 1989 to 2002. It was a turbocharged version of the SR20DE and a replacement for the CA18DET. The SR20DET, like the CA18DET, was equipped with a turbocharger and intercooler. It was a popular four-cylinder engine found in various Nissan vehicles, most notably the Nissan Silvia and 180SX.
